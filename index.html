01. About Me

Hello, I’m Paarth Parekh.I’m passionate about transforming complex biological data into actionable insights through computational modeling and bioinformatics. My journey began when I realized that raw omics data could be translated into models that could drive disease modeling, predict drug behavior, and guide clinical decisions for drug development.

With experience ranging from established biotech environments to innovative start-ups, I've honed skills in multi-omics integration, data pipeline optimization, and predictive modeling. Currently, I'm focused on developing computational tools and client-facing software to streamline drug discovery and improve in vitro-to-clinical translation, working closely with cross-functional teams.

Outside of work, I enjoy cooking, experimenting with new recipes, and traveling to explore diverse cultures and destinations.

Here are a few areas I’ve been working on recently:

  • Multi-omics data integration and analysis.
  • ODE-based modeling for drug kinetics.
  • Genome-scale metabolic models for target discovery.
  • Automated data pipelines for rapid, reproducible research.
